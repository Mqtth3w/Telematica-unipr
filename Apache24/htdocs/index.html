White Paper

The purpose of the following white paper is to introduce ConnectionGOAT as a scientific article publishing channel. It is a database of scientific articles, guidelines, papers, best practices that others can use as a basis for future standards, certifications, laws, policies or as standards themselves.
The aim is to allow anyone to publish their scientific work without too much difficulty.
The features presented are focused on the fact that everyone can collaborate and enrich the scientific knowledge we have. The service is the result of a university project concerning telematics created by the working group Donato Bruno & Matteo Gianvenuti, future engineers working for the scientific community. The group intends to provide a platform for discussions and follow-up activities resulting from the events of the scientific community, experts and non-experts mainly in the technological field in any case any type of work can be published giving the community the task of evaluating the works published by expressing their positive or negative opinion. The site intends to give wide visibility to published scientific works. Ultimately it allows the following:
• Ability to access the site having first registered.
• Ability to log out of the site after an authenticated access, of course.
• Possibility of registering on the site in order to be able to participate and give one's contribution to the scientific community, with a warm welcome email.
• Ability to send an email to developers contributing to the development and improvement of the site by providing development suggestions.
• Possibility of voting on articles and subsequently viewing the current scores, after authenticated access.
• Possibility of inserting a new scientific work into the database by supplying the necessary data.
• Ability to search for a scientific article in the database and then view it.
